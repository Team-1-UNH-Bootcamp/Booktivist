Why I created Kid Booktvist

My family has been intentional in collecting diverse books for our kids bookshelves that celebrate differences, highlight people of color and women as leaders, and explain the large range of gender identities and presentations.

We want to continue to grow our collection, and sometimes it is hard to know which books to choose when you can’t page through the books in a physical bookstore. This is why I created this site. I want parents to be able to find books that open their children’s eyes to honoring differences and to activism. I want parents and their children to be able to review the books before deciding which to purchase. I want parents and caregivers to be able to purchase these books with confidence and excitement at black-owned bookstores, queer-owned bookstores, and independent bookstores so they will become bedtime favorites. I hope this helps you to share these videos with your children and have the tough conversations we all need to be having right now. - Jess Samuels Mom to 2 kiddos

-Jess Samuels
